## 模块一：思维重构

### 1.1 论文本质认知
- 论文不是"总结"，而是**说服**——SCI论文的本质是围绕一个明确问题构建证据链，最终说服读者接受你的结论
- Nature级论文的核心：**一个清晰问题 + 一个关键结论**，其余所有内容都服务于这条主线，而非与它平起平坐
- 禁止思维：内容越多越好——审稿人只关心内容是否支持一个清晰的结论，信息过多但缺乏主线会被评价为"descriptive but lacks focus"；结构越复杂越高级——复杂 ≠ 高级，清晰 = 高级

### 1.2 中式思维 vs 英文科研思维
- 中文思维：铺垫→转折→结论
- 英文思维：**结论先行→证据支撑**
- 核心转变：从"记录我做了什么" → "说服别人接受我的结论"。写的不是工作流程，而是**论证过程**，组织的不是实验顺序，而是**逻辑顺序**，关心的不是"我写全了吗"，而是"读者被说服了吗"
- 关键规则：避免"Although..., but..."结构
  - ❌ Although A is important, but B is more critical.
  - ✅ Although A is important, we find that B is more critical.

#### 中式思维的四类典型迁移问题
1. **背景前置过度**：先写大量条件、背景、现状，再迟迟给出核心判断
2. **让步结构滥用**：以 although / while / despite 为主要推进方式，句子总在"解释前提"而非"推进论点"
3. **评价模糊化**：a certain degree / relatively / to some extent，以为更客观，实际削弱命题可证伪性
4. **作者立场隐匿**：不敢写 we show / we demonstrate / we find，退回被动、抽象、无主语表达，论证主体消失

#### 关键规则：避免让步结构压制主命题
- ❌ Although A is important, but B is more critical.（语法错误）- ❌ Although A, we found B.（A 篇幅过长、B 表达模糊，主信息仍被降格）- ✅ Although A is important, we find that B is more critical.- ✅ A remains limited in B.（直接命中问题，无需让步）- ✅ Despite its widespread use, A does not resolve B.（让步简短，主张清晰）
> 核心原则：让让步结构只承担**限定功能**，让主句承担**论证功能**；真正重要的发现应落在主句，不埋在从句之后

#### 段落组织：逻辑单元而非材料集合
- 英文科研段落 = 小型论证模块，遵循 **主张→证据→解释**- 段首句 = 本段的 proposition statement，不是泛泛引入- 段落不是"材料集合"，而是"逻辑单元"；没有段首主张，段落就沦为信息堆积

#### 四维自检标准
1. **主命题识别度**：读者能否在句前半段就知道你真正要说什么
2. **句法主干承载度**：最重要信息是否出现在主句而非从句
3. **逻辑方向清晰度**：这句话是在提出发现、限定条件、解释机制，还是与已有研究对比
4. **证据挂靠明确度**：后续信息是否明确支持当前命题，而非横向发散

#### claim strength 与 evidential support 必须匹配
- 命题越明确，证据越要精准；结论越直接，限定语越要恰当- hedge devices 不是让句子变软，而是让结论与证据强度匹配：suggest / indicate / is associated with / may contribute to / is consistent with

#### Chinese English 的本质
- 不是口音问题，不是词汇问题，而是**信息组织方式仍服从中文思维，未进入英文科研论证框架**- 英文科研写作 = 把中文式铺垫逻辑改造为英文式论证逻辑- 目标：**主张可见、证据可追、逻辑可审**

### 1.3 逻辑优先原则
- 论文质量 ≠ 词汇难度,审稿人评价的是"你说得清不清楚"，不是"你词汇有多高级"
- Nature论文特点：用词简单，结构极强
  结论前置（We find that… 而非 After analyzing…, we found…）、
  信息密度极高（一句话只做一件事，但信息很"硬"）、
  句子之间是推进关系而非并列关系
- 说服力的来源 = **一个清晰的问题 + 一个明确的结论 + 一条严密的逻辑链**
- **核心原则：One sentence = One idea**，一个句子只承载一个信息点，禁止在一个句子中堆叠多个逻辑
 - 句子逻辑三种基本类型：**结论句（Claim）→ 证据句（Evidence）→ 解释句（Interpretation）​**
- 顶级论文的共同特征：结论出现得非常早，句子结构简洁直接， 每一段都有明确功能

#### 词汇简单 vs 词汇复杂的对比
❌ The results demonstrate a significant amelioration in system performance.
✅ The results significantly improve system performance.
> 信息相同，但第二句更快被理解

#### 复杂词汇掩盖逻辑的对比
❌ Various factors potentially contribute to the observed phenomenon.（什么因素？怎么贡献？没说清）
✅ Temperature increase leads to higher reaction rates.
> 逻辑清晰 > 语言高级

#### Nature风格典型结构
We show that X increases Y. This effect is driven by Z. These findings suggest A.
> 每句一个结论，逻辑递进清晰，无多余修饰

#### One sentence = One idea 拆解示例
❌ In this study, we propose a novel method which is efficient and robust and can handle large-scale data and also outperforms previous methods.（4个信息塞在一句话里，没有主次，审稿人抓不到重点）
✅ 逐步拆解：1. We propose a novel method.（提出方法）2. The method is efficient and robust.（性能特点）3. It handles large-scale data.（应用范围）4. It outperforms previous methods.（对比优势）
✅ 高级压缩版：We propose a robust and efficient method that outperforms existing approaches on large-scale data.
> 可以合并，但逻辑必须清晰；不能让读者"解码句子"

#### 句子推进关系 vs 并列关系
❌ We tested A. We tested B. We tested C.（简单并列，没有推进）
✅ We first tested A. We then examined B to validate this effect. Finally, we confirmed C.
> 每句话都在"推动故事前进"

#### 段落标准逻辑链示例
Claim → Evidence → Interpretation
We find that X increases Y. → This is supported by Figure 2. → This suggests that Z drives the effect.
> 结论 → 证据 → 解释，三层递进构成完整论证

#### 删除"无信息词"示例
❌ It is worth noting that the results show…
✅ The results show…
> 删掉"It is worth noting that""It can be seen that""As is well known"等废话，直接给出信息
 
### 1.4 读者视角
**核心转变：写作时不要问"我想说什么？"，而要问"审稿人想看到什么？"​**
审稿人最关心三个问题（写作全程必须围绕此三问）：
1. 你解决了什么问题？（What problem）
 - 开篇即亮剑：Introduction第一段末尾或第二段，必须明确写出 "Here, we address the problem of..." 或 "A key unresolved question is..."  
 - 不要铺垫过长，审稿人不需要从头科普
2. 为什么重要？（Why it matters）
 - 量化重要性：不要只说"很重要"，要说清楚——性能提升10倍？解决十年争议？开辟新方向？
 - 在多处强调：Abstract末尾、Introduction末尾、Discussion开头
3. 为什么可信？（Why we should believe you）
  - 主动提供证据链：实验数据 + 对照实验 + 统计分析 + 理论支持
  - 预判质疑并回应：在Results或Discussion中主动提出可能的反驳并解答
  - 方法部分必须详细到"可重复"

#### 正确 vs 错误案例
**问题1：开篇是否快速点题**
- ❌ "Lithium-ion batteries were invented in 1991. They have many advantages. They are used in phones, laptops, and electric cars.（写了一大段，还没提出问题）"
- ✅ "While lithium-ion batteries are widely used, their degradation under fast-charging remains poorly understood. **Here, we solve this problem by revealing a new degradation mechanism.​**"

**问题2：重要性是否量化且具体**
- ❌ "This result is very important and interesting."
- ✅ "This new mechanism not only explains the long-observed capacity fade but also enables a simple electrolyte design that could extend battery life by 50%."

**问题3：可信度是否有证据支撑**
- ❌ "We believe the catalyst worked well. The result seems reliable."
- ✅ "To confirm the catalytic role of the Pt nanoparticles, we conducted a control experiment without Pt, which showed no reaction (Fig. 2b). Statistical analysis of 100 independent measurements yielded p < 0.001."

禁止行为：
- **自嗨式写作**（只顾展示自己做了多少工作，把论文写成"成果展示清单"）
 - 80/20法则：80%的工作（条件摸索、重复验证、方法优化）属于Methods或Supplementary Information；只有20%直接回答核心问题的决定性发现才应出现在Results中

- **背景过长**（铺垫超过必要篇幅，读者前几段仍无法回答"这篇论文到底在讲什么"）
 - 采用倒三角结构：第1段大背景（2-3句）→ 第2段研究现状与gap → 第3段本文工作（We, Here, In this study）——**这是唯一重要的一段**

**审稿人模拟清单**（在写完每一个段落（尤其是Introduction和Discussion）后，请用这个清单自问）：
| 你写的句子 | 审稿人可能的追问 | 应对策略 |
|---        |---              |---      |
| "We performed experiment A…" | "So what？结论是什么？" | 不要只写做了什么，立刻写出发现 |
| "Our result shows a difference…" | "How big？统计显著吗？" | 给出量化信息，如 "a 50% reduction (p<0.001, n=10)" |
| "This suggests mechanism M…" | "Why not mechanism N？" | 主动提供对照实验排除其他可能 |
| "This finding is important…" | "具体用在哪儿？" | 给出具体场景，如 "for designing longer-lasting EV batteries" |
| （写了一长段背景） | "你的贡献在哪儿？" | 结论先行，把 "Here, we…" 提到段首或明确标出 |

## 模块二：结构训练

### 2.1 IMRaD标准结构
每一部分有且仅有一个任务：

| 部分 | 唯一任务 | 禁止行为 |
|---|---|---|
| Introduction | 提出问题→揭示空白→声明贡献 | 大段背景堆砌、不聚焦 |
| Methods | 提供可复现的实验路径 | 解释为什么选此方法（放Discussion） |
| Results | 呈现发现 | 夹带解释和讨论 |
| Discussion | 阐释意义、比较、局限 | 重复Results内容 |

| 部分 | 唯一任务 | 禁止行为 |
|---|---|---|
| Introduction | 建立研究缺口（Establish the gap）：大背景→研究现状→研究缺口→本文工作 | 大段背景堆砌、罗列文献、在引言中报告具体结果 |
| Methods | 提供可重复性（Enable reproducibility）：试剂、参数、流程、统计 | 解释方法选择理由、炫耀仪器、提前泄露结果 |
| Results | 呈现关键发现（Present key findings）：一段一结论、图驱动、只报不解释 | 夹带解释讨论（suggests/because/important）、重复实验流程 |
| Discussion | 解释意义与局限（Interpret & contextualize）：意味着什么→与文献比较→局限与展望 | 重复Results数据、空泛吹嘘、回避局限 |


#### Introduction 四步漏斗结构
1. **大背景**（1-2句）：该领域为何重要
2. **研究现状**（3-5句）：别人做了什么
3. **研究缺口**（1-2句，最关键）：However / Yet / Despite these advances… → 还有什么没解决
4. **本文工作**（2-3句）：我们做了什么来填补缺口，以及核心发现
> ✅ "Perovskite solar cells have achieved efficiencies over 25%. **However**, their operational stability under real-world conditions remains a major barrier. **Here, we demonstrate that a novel 2D/3D interfacial structure extends device lifetime by 10-fold.​**"
> ❌ "Perovskite solar cells were first reported in 2009. Since then, many groups have worked on them…（两页文献列表）…In this paper, we also did some experiments on stability." — 无清晰gap

#### Methods 黄金标准
- 足够详细：试剂纯度、设备型号、参数设置、统计方法
- 逻辑顺序：按实验流程写，不跳跃
- 被动语态可接受（IMRaD中唯一合理使用被动语态的部分）
> ✅ "The solution was spin-coated on ITO substrates at 4000 rpm for 30 s, followed by annealing at 100°C for 10 min in a nitrogen glovebox (O₂ < 0.1 ppm, H₂O < 0.1 ppm)."
> ❌ "We made the perovskite films using a standard method. Then we measured them with some equipment." — 无法重复

#### Results 核心原则
- **一段一结论**：段首Topic sentence概括核心发现
- **图驱动**：先设计图表，再围绕图表写文字
- **只报不解释**：描述趋势、对比数值、指出相关性，但不解释机制或意义
- **数据+统计**：所有关键结论必须有数据支撑
> ✅ "The degradation rate is strongly temperature-dependent. At 25°C, the device retained 90% efficiency after 1000 h. At 60°C, it dropped to 50% within 100 h (Fig. 3a)."
> ❌ "…This is probably because the high temperature causes defects to form. This is very important for applications." — 混入解释（probably because）和吹意义（very important）

#### Discussion 倒漏斗结构
1. **重申核心发现**（1-2句，简要，不复制Results）
2. **解释意义**：发现为什么重要？改变了什么认知？
3. **与文献比较**：支持/反对/补充了已有文献？为什么？
4. **局限性与展望**：主动承认不足，提出未来方向
> ✅ "Unlike previous reports that focused on bulk composition engineering, our work identifies interfacial morphology as a dominant factor. However, this study is limited to lab-scale devices; future work should test real-world solar profiles."
> ❌ "In summary, at 25°C the device is stable, and at 60°C it degrades. Our work is the best. There are no limitations." — 重复Results、空泛吹嘘、不承认局限


### 2.2 段落结构（最重要的规则）
每个段落必须遵循三段式：
1. **Topic Sentence**（主题句）——段落灵魂，用一句话**完整、明确**地给出本段核心结论，放在**第一句**   
- 必须是**结论性**的，不是"我们做了X"，而是"X导致了Y"   
- 必须**可验证**：后面的证据能直接支撑这句话   
- 必须**独立可读**：只读所有段落首句就能理解全文逻辑

2. **Evidence**（证据）——用具体数据/实验/文献支撑主题句，占中间2-5句   
- 数据证据：具体数字和比较（"increased by 30%"，而非"was better"）   
- 统计证据：p值、样本量、检验方法   
- 图像/文献证据：引用图表或前人研究   
- 证据按**逻辑顺序**排列（从宏观到微观、从直接到间接），而非时间顺序

3. **Interpretation**（解释/过渡）——说明证据意义或为下一段铺垫   
- **Results部分**：弱解释，仅指出趋势或比较，不深入机制   
- **Introduction/Discussion部分**：强解释，必须解释意义、联系更大问题、引出局限

**核心原则：一个段落 = 一个核心观点（One paragraph = One idea）​**
禁止：
- 没有主题句的段落（以"We measured…"、"Figure X shows…"开头）
- 纯信息堆叠的段落（多个不相关观点挤在一段内）
- 以背景/铺垫开头的段落（背景只在Introduction中）- 结论埋在段尾甚至缺失

#### 段落自检清单
| 检查项 | 若不通过则 |
|---|---|
| 本段是否只有一个核心观点？ | 拆分成两个段落 |
| 第一句是否直接给出结论？ | 把结论移到句首，删除"We measured…"式开头 |
| 后面的句子是否都支撑该结论？ | 删除跑题内容 |
| 证据是否具体（有数字、比较、统计）？ | 把"better"改成"30% higher" |
| 段尾是否有解释或过渡（如需要）？ | 加上"These results indicate…"或过渡句 |
| 只读第一句能知道整段在说什么吗？ | 强化主题句使其自包含 |

#### Results段落示例
**​【主题句】​** The 2D/3D interfacial structure significantly enhances device stability. **​【证据1】​** Devices with the 2D/3D interface retained 92% of their initial efficiency after 1000 h of continuous illumination, whereas control devices retained only 45% under identical conditions (Fig. 4a). **​【证据2】​** The enhanced stability was reproducible across 20 independent devices, with a standard deviation of less than 5% (Fig. 4b). **​【弱解释/过渡】​** These results indicate that the 2D/3D interface, rather than other factors, is responsible for the improved operational lifetime.

#### Introduction段落示例
**​【主题句】​** Despite advances in perovskite efficiency, the operational stability of these devices remains a critical barrier to commercialization. **​【证据/背景】​** Several strategies have been explored, including compositional engineering (ref 5-7) and encapsulation (ref 8-9), yet the average device lifetime under real-world conditions rarely exceeds 1000 h. **​【解释/引出gap】​** The lack of stability is primarily attributed to ion migration and interfacial degradation, but the exact degradation pathways are still poorly understood. **​【过渡】​** Here, we address this gap by designing a 2D/3D interface that suppresses ion migration.


### 2.3 逻辑连接规则
核心原则：写作时不要问"我下一个要说什么"，而要问"上下句之间是什么逻辑关系"——是因果、递进、转折还是并列？只有纯粹并列时才勉强可用 First/Second，而大多数情况下，观点之间的关系更丰富，应选择精确的逻辑连接词。
禁止使用列表式连接词：
- ❌ First, Second, Third...
- ❌ Firstly, Secondly, Thirdly...
- ❌ "Three factors explain... First, ... Second, ... Third, ..." 式预告+列表
列表式连接词的问题：传递"只会并列堆砌，不会逻辑推进"的信号，掩盖了观点间的真实逻辑关系。

替代策略：
- 逻辑递进：more importantly, notably, crucially, furthermore, moreover
- 因果结构：therefore, consequently, as a result, thus, hence, which in turn
- 转折强调：yet, instead, nevertheless, in contrast, conversely, whereas
- 时间顺序（方法部分）：subsequently, followed by, prior to
- 结论归纳：overall, collectively, taken together, in summary

#### 因果链替代列表
> ❌ "First, we increased the annealing temperature. Second, the grain size grew larger. Third, the device efficiency improved."
> ✅ "Increasing the annealing temperature **led to** larger grain sizes, **which in turn** improved device efficiency. **Thus,​** higher annealing temperature enhances performance through grain growth."

#### 递进结构替代列表
> ❌ "First, our catalyst shows high activity. Second, it is stable. Third, it is low-cost."
> ✅ "Our catalyst exhibits high activity. **More importantly,​** it maintains this activity over 100 cycles. **Furthermore,​** its low-cost synthesis makes it practical for scale-up."

#### 转折/对比替代列表
> ❌ "First, method A gave 80% yield. Second, method B gave only 50% yield."
> ✅ "Method A achieved 80% yield. **In contrast,​** method B yielded only 50% under identical conditions."

#### 彻底重构"三点式"段落
> ❌ "Three factors explain the enhanced stability. First, the 2D layer blocks moisture. Second, it suppresses ion migration. Third, it reduces interfacial defects."
> ✅ "The enhanced stability arises from multiple synergistic effects of the 2D layer. **Primarily,​** it acts as a physical barrier against moisture ingress. **In addition,​** the 2D layer suppresses ion migration, a major degradation pathway. **Crucially,​** it also passivates interfacial defects, which further prevents recombination and degradation."

要点：删掉预告句；用 Primarily / In addition / Crucially 实现递进，Crucially 放最后暗示最重要；每个连接词都传达逻辑关系而非序号。
自检规则：写完后搜索 "First"、"Second"、"Third"，若出现超过两次，认真考虑改写。


### 2.4 故事线设计

整篇论文必须围绕一条主线：

**Question → Gap → Method → Key finding → Impact**

| 步骤 | 要素 | 要回答的问题 | 对应论文部分 |
|------|------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | Question | 我们要解决什么大问题？ | Introduction（背景） |
| 2 | Gap | 现有研究还缺什么？ | Introduction（缺口） |
| 3 | Method | 我们用什么方法来解决？ | Methods / Introduction末尾 |
| 4 | Key finding | 我们发现了什么核心结果？ | Results / Abstract |
| 5 | Impact | 这个发现为什么重要？ | Discussion / Abstract末尾 |

禁止：多个并列点拼凑，缺乏统一叙事。

#### 核心原则

- 一篇论文 = 一个故事 = 一条主线，不是多个实验结果的堆砌
- 动笔前先填五要素表格，填不出说明故事没想清楚，不要动笔
- 核心答案必须具体且有结论（错误："我们研究了钙钛矿太阳能电池"；正确："引入2D/3D界面结构可将器件寿命延长10倍"）

#### 常见问题与对策

- **塞太多点** → 砍掉次要结果移至Supplementary，只留一个核心贡献。问自己："如果只能保留一个发现，我留哪一个？"
- **故事线隐藏** → 结论前置，Abstract首句亮出问题和核心答案，Introduction末尾明确写出"Here, we …"
- **多个发现逻辑混乱** → 用连接词（Consistent with / Notably / In contrast / Taken together）明确发现间是支持、补充还是挑战主线的关系

#### 案例：钙钛矿太阳能电池论文

- **核心问题：​** 钙钛矿太阳能电池不稳定，无法商业化
- **现有缺口：​** 大家都做组分工程，但界面问题被忽视
- **我们的方法：​** 设计2D/3D界面结构
- **核心发现：​** 器件寿命从100小时延长到1000小时（10倍）
- **重要意义：​** 提供新的界面工程策略，可用于商业化

Abstract一句话版本：

> The operational stability of perovskite solar cells remains a critical barrier to commercialization. **Here, we show that a 2D/3D interfacial structure extends device lifetime by 10-fold, addressing the overlooked issue of interfacial degradation.​**

## 模块三：句子级优化

### 3.1 Chinese English问题清单

诊断以下典型问题：

| 问题类型 | ❌ 错误示例 | ✅ 正确改写 |
|---|---|---|
| 虽然但是 | Although X, but Y | Although X, Y（删but）|
| 因为所以 | Because X, so Y | Because X, Y（删so）|
| 冗余表达 | It is worth noting that X | Notably, X 或直接陈述 |
| 被动滥用 | It was observed that X increased | X increased / Fig.2 shows X |
| 名词化 | make an improvement | improve |
| 空洞填充 | In order to / Due to the fact that | To / Because |
| 弱动词 | make a contribution to | contribute to |
| 模糊堆叠 | might possibly indicate that | suggests / indicates（保留1个）|
| There be开头 | There is a significant difference between X and Y | X differs significantly from Y |
| 重复修饰 | completely eliminate | eliminate |

#### 案例一：虽然但是 / 因为所以

❌ "Although the device showed high efficiency, **but** its stability was poor."
❌ "Because the temperature increased, **so** the reaction rate doubled."
✅ "Although the device showed high efficiency, its stability was poor."
✅ "The temperature increased, **so** the reaction rate doubled."

> Although与but、Because与so不能同时出现，二者只选其一。

#### 案例二：冗余表达

❌ "It is worth noting that, due to the fact that the temperature was increased to 150°C, there was a significant increase in the reaction yield."
✅ "Increasing the temperature to 150°C significantly increased the reaction yield."

> 常见替换：It should be emphasized that → Importantly；In the present study, we → Here, we；At a temperature of 150°C → At 150°C

#### 案例三：被动滥用

❌ "The samples were prepared by the authors. The measurements were performed using an SEM. It was found that the temperature had an effect."
✅ "We prepared the samples and measured them using SEM. Temperature affected the reaction yield."

> Methods部分被动可接受；Results/Discussion尽量用主动。

#### 案例四：名词化

❌ "We **made a measurement** of the temperature and **performed an analysis** of the data."
✅ "We **measured** the temperature and **analyzed** the data."

> 常见替换：conduct an analysis → analyze；reach a conclusion → conclude；give a demonstration → demonstrate

#### 案例五：空洞填充

❌ "In order to confirm this hypothesis, due to the fact that X, for the purpose of Y..."
✅ "To confirm this hypothesis, because X, for Y..."

> 能删则删：In order to → To；Due to the fact that → Because；For the purpose of → For/To

#### 案例六：弱动词

❌ "We **made a measurement** and **gave a comparison** between the two samples and **reached a conclusion** that the first one is better."
✅ "We **measured** and **compared** the two samples, **concluding** that the first one is better."

> 用强动词替代"轻动词+名词"结构，句子更直接有力。

#### 案例七：模糊堆叠

❌ "This result **might indicate** that temperature **could have an effect** on the reaction."
✅ 证据充分 → "Temperature **affects** the reaction."
✅ 证据较强 → "Our results **suggest** that temperature influences the reaction."

> 避免might/maybe/possibly堆叠，根据证据强度选1个程度词即可。

#### 案例八：There be开头

❌ "There is a significant difference between the efficiency of the device fabricated at 150°C and the device fabricated at 100°C."
✅ "The 150°C device **outperformed** the 100°C device in efficiency."

> 主语短小、结论前置，让动词尽早出现。

#### 综合改写

❌ "It is worth noting that, due to the fact that the temperature was increased to a higher level, there was a significant increase in the reaction yield. Although the yield increased, but the selectivity decreased. There is a possibility that this phenomenon might be caused by the thermal decomposition of the catalyst."

✅ "Higher temperature increased yield but decreased selectivity, suggesting thermal decomposition of the catalyst."

> 自检口诀：能删则删、能主动则主动、能动词则动词、能直接则直接。



### 3.2 短句原则

- 平均句长控制在**15–20词**（Nature平均水平）
- 超过**25词**的句子必须拆解，超过**30词**必须拆解
- 拆解方法：找出句子中的多个逻辑点，每个逻辑点独立成句

#### 三条核心原则

1. **一句话 = 一个想法**：每多一个"and""which""that""while""whereas"，就考虑是否拆开
2. **主谓宾靠前**：主语和动词之间不超过5个词，修饰成分后置或删除
3. **删掉一切不必要的词**：每删一个词，句子就强一分

#### 常见冗余词速删表

| 可删词/短语 | 替换为 | 示例 |
|---|---|---|
| very, really, quite, rather | 直接删除 | "very high" → "high" |
| in order to | to | "in order to improve" → "to improve" |
| it is important that | 直接陈述事实 | 删除整个短语 |
| as shown in Fig. 2 | 括号引用 | "as shown in Fig. 2, the yield increased" → "The yield increased (Fig. 2)" |
| respectively | 多数可删 | "A and B were 10 and 20, respectively" → "A was 10, B was 20" |
| due to the fact that | because | — |
| in the case of | for | "in the case of sample A" → "for sample A" |

#### 拆解案例一：一句话塞多个想法

❌ *We increased the annealing temperature to 150°C, which led to larger grains, and the larger grains then improved the device efficiency, so we concluded that higher temperature is beneficial.*

✅ *Increasing the annealing temperature to 150°C produced larger grains. These larger grains improved device efficiency, indicating that higher temperature is beneficial.*

#### 拆解案例二：主谓之间插入过长修饰

❌ *The perovskite film, which was prepared using a two-step method that involved a 30-minute annealing step at 150°C followed by a cooling step, showed high efficiency.*

✅ *The perovskite film showed high efficiency. It was prepared using a two-step method (150°C annealing for 30 min followed by cooling).*

#### 拆解案例三：which/that从句嵌套

❌ *We used a new measurement technique, which allows us to probe the interface with nanometer resolution, which was not possible with conventional methods.*

✅ *We used a new measurement technique. It probes the interface with nanometer resolution. Conventional methods cannot achieve this resolution.*

#### 拆解案例四：修饰词堆砌

❌ *We observed a very significantly enhanced and extremely high photocatalytic activity.*

✅ *We observed high photocatalytic activity.*（用量化数据支撑：The degradation rate was 0.05 min⁻¹.）

#### 句子强度自检表

| 检查项 | 理想值 | 超标处理 |
|---|---|---|
| 词数 | 15–20词 | >25词 → 拆分 |
| "and"数量 | 0–1个 | ≥2个 → 拆分或列表 |
| "which/that"从句 | 0–1个 | ≥2个 → 拆成独立句 |
| 主谓之间词数 | ≤5词 | >5词 → 调整语序 |
| 冗余词 | 0个 | >0个 → 删除 |


### 3.3 连接词升级

> **核心原则：​** 连接词不是装饰，是逻辑的翻译器。用错了或滥用了，反而混淆逻辑。只有当逻辑关系可能被误解时，才使用显式连接词；当逻辑足够清晰时，让内容自己说话（零连接词）。

| 降级（避免过度使用） | 升级（推荐替代） | 逻辑关系 |
|---|---|---|
| however | Yet, In contrast, Nevertheless, Conversely | 转折/对比 |
| moreover | Notably, Importantly, Crucially, Significantly | 递进/补充 |
| in addition | Furthermore, Additionally | 简单补充 |
| therefore | Consequently, Accordingly, As a result, Hence | 因果/结果 |
| but | Instead, Whereas | 否定替代/句中对比 |

#### however → Yet / In contrast / Nevertheless

- **Yet**：中等语气，引出意外对比
- **In contrast**：强语气，明确对比两个事物
- **Nevertheless**：强语气正式，尽管前面有问题仍然……

❌ "The first method is fast. **However,​** it gives low yield. The second method is slow. **However,​** it gives high yield."

✅ "The first method is fast but gives low yield. **In contrast,​** the second method is slow **yet** produces high yield."

#### moreover → Notably / Importantly / Crucially

- **Notably**：强调特别值得注意的点
- **Importantly**：强调重要性
- **Crucially**：强调关键/决定性因素

❌ "The catalyst shows high activity. **Moreover,​** it is stable. **Moreover,​** it is selective."

✅ "The catalyst shows high activity. **Notably,​** it remains stable over 100 cycles. **Importantly,​** it is also selective for the desired product."

#### therefore → Consequently / As a result / Accordingly

- **Consequently**：强调直接后果
- **As a result**：自然常用
- **Accordingly**：基于前面推理采取行动

❌ "The temperature increased. **Therefore,​** the reaction rate increased. **Therefore,​** the yield increased."

✅ "Increasing the temperature accelerated the reaction rate, which in turn increased the yield. **Thus,​** temperature is a key parameter."

#### 零连接词（最高级衔接）

当逻辑足够清晰时，不需要显式连接词：

✅ "We increased the annealing temperature to 150°C. The grains grew larger. Device efficiency improved from 18% to 22%."（读者自动理解因果链）

**自检方法：​** 写完后搜索 However、Moreover、Therefore、Furthermore，若每个出现超过 3 次，则替换或删除。



### 3.4 动词驱动写作

核心规则：**去名词化，动词优先**

动词是句子的引擎，引擎越强，句子越有力。名词化（Nominalization）= 弱动词 + 名词，会让句子变长、变弱、变模糊。

| ❌ 名词化表达 | ✅ 动词驱动表达 |
|---|---|
| make an improvement | improve |
| conduct an investigation | investigate |
| make a comparison | compare |
| provide an explanation | explain |
| reach a conclusion | conclude |
| give a description | describe |
| have a discussion | discuss |
| perform an analysis | analyze |
| do a calculation | calculate |
| take a measurement | measure |
| have an effect on | affect |
| show an increase | increase |
| undergo a change | change |
| be in agreement with | agree with |
| be dependent on | depend on |
| be indicative of | indicate |
| be different from | differ from |
| There is a need for | We need / Requires |
| There was an increase | Increased |

#### 三类名词化问题及改写

1. **make / do / have + 名词** → 删除弱动词，把名词变动词
2. **there be + 名词化** → 删除 there be，把名词变动词，找到真正主语
3. **be + 形容词 + 名词化** → 删除 be 和名词，变形容词为动词

#### "X of Y" 结构改写

把名词改回动词，反转语序：Y 动词化

| ❌ of 结构泛滥 | ✅ 动词驱动 |
|---|---|
| The degradation of the device was observed | The device degraded |
| The addition of the catalyst led to an increase in the yield | Adding the catalyst increased the yield |
| We made a comparison of the two methods | We compared the two methods |

#### 强动词选择：根据证据强度选词

不要过度夸张，也不要过分保守，根据证据强度选择合适动词：

| 弱动词 | 中等动词 | 强动词 |
|---|---|---|
| is / are | becomes / remains | 直接描述 |
| has / there is | exhibits / shows | demonstrates / reveals |
| affects | influences | determines / governs / drives |
| increases | enhances | boosts / accelerates / amplifies |
| decreases | reduces | suppresses / inhibits / eliminates |
| is important | matters | dominates / dictates |

#### 替代 "shows" 的精确动词

| 情境 | 可替代动词 |
|---|---|
| 揭示新发现 | reveals, uncovers, demonstrates |
| 证明因果关系 | establishes, confirms, verifies |
| 对比差异 | contrasts, distinguishes, differentiates |
| 指出趋势 | indicates, suggests, implies（较弱） |
| 主导/决定 | governs, dictates, determines, drives |
| 抑制/促进 | suppresses, promotes, facilitates, accelerates |

#### 自检方法

写完每个句子后问自己：**​"这个句子的核心动作是什么？那个动作是以动词形式出现的吗？"​** 如果核心动作是名词形式，就改写。

### 4.1 Introduction写作模板

结构（4步，共3-4段）：

**第1段：大背景（1-2句）​**

> [领域] plays a crucial role in [更广泛的意义].

规则：
- 直接切入子领域，不要宽到"能源是人类社会发展的基础"这种程度
- 用1-2句话告诉读者这个领域为什么重要，但不展开

**第2段：研究现状 + 研究空白（5-8句）​**

> However, [已知A], [已知B], yet [关键未知/矛盾] remains unclear.

结构：
1. 概述领域进展（2-4句，引用关键文献）
2. 指出缺口（1-2句，用 However/Yet/Despite… 开头）
3. 可选：为什么这个缺口重要（1句）

缺口表达模板：
- "However, little is known about…"
- "Despite these advances, the role of X remains unclear."
- "A key unresolved question is whether…"
- "The mechanism by which X affects Y has not been established."

规则：
- Gap必须具体、可验证，不能用"more research is needed"等模糊表述
- "However"或"Yet"应在第2-3段就出现，不要在背景中绕太久

**第3段：本文解决问题（2-3句）​**

> Here, we investigate [核心问题] by [方法概述].

规则：
- 以"Here / In this study / We"明确写出本文工作
- 用具体动词：design, synthesize, measure, investigate, reveal, demonstrate
- 在第2段或第3段末尾就亮出"Here, we…"，不要写太晚

**第4段：核心贡献（1-2句）​**

> We show that [关键发现]. This finding [意义/影响].

规则：
- 给出具体数字和对比（如"10-fold"、"22.5%"）
- 最后一句话必须是本文最重要的结论
- 注意：Nature等部分期刊要求Introduction末尾不要过度预告结果

#### 完整案例：钙钛矿太阳能电池稳定性研究

**​(Step 1: 大背景)​** Perovskite solar cells have achieved power conversion efficiencies exceeding 25%, positioning them as a leading next-generation photovoltaic technology.

**​(Step 2: 研究现状+缺口)​** Much of this progress has come from compositional engineering of the perovskite absorber (ref 1–3) and optimization of charge-transport layers (ref 4–6). **However,​** the operational stability of these devices under real-world conditions remains a critical barrier to commercialization. **In particular,​** degradation at the perovskite/charge-transport layer interface is poorly understood, and existing strategies—such as encapsulation or additive engineering—have not yet resolved this issue (ref 7–9).

**​(Step 3: 本文工作)​** **Here,​** we design a 2D/3D perovskite interface to suppress interfacial degradation. By systematically varying the 2D layer thickness and using in situ photoluminescence mapping, we investigate how this interface affects charge extraction, ion migration, and device stability.

**​(Step 4: 贡献预告)​** We find that an optimized 2D/3D interface extends the device operational lifetime by 10-fold (from 100 h to >1000 h) while maintaining a power conversion efficiency of 22.5%. **These results identify interfacial degradation as a key bottleneck and provide a design principle for stable perovskite photovoltaics.​**

#### 自检要点

- Step 1 是否在2-3句话内给出了大背景？→ 删掉废话，从子领域直接切入
- Step 2 是否明确指出了**具体的**研究缺口？→ 把"more research is needed"改成具体问题
- Step 2 中"However"是否在第2-3段就出现了？→ 快速进入缺口
- Step 3 是否以"Here/In this study/We"明确写出本文工作？→ 加上明确过渡词和具体动词
- Step 4 是否给出了具体的核心发现（数字/对比）？→ 加上关键结果
- 整篇Introduction是否超过800词？→ 删减背景，只保留必要信息
- 是否每句都有明确功能（背景/进展/缺口/本文/贡献）？→ 删除凑字数的句子


### 4.2 Results写作模板

#### 核心原则

- **一段一结论**：每段的主题句就是该段的核心发现，不要以"Figure X shows…"或"We measured…"开头
- **只写发现，不写解释**：避免解释性动词（suggests, indicates, implies）和意义性陈述（is important, has implications for）；如需关联可用弱表达（correlates with, is consistent with），深入解释留给Discussion
- **数据呈现后紧跟比较与统计**：每个关键结论须附样本量（n）、误差（SD/SEM）、统计显著性（p值）
- **图驱动写作**：先设计图表逻辑顺序，再围绕图表写文字；文字引导读者关注图中关键信息，而非重复图表所有数字
- **按逻辑顺序而非实验时间顺序组织**：可选用宏观→微观、现象→机制证据、核心发现→辅助证据等逻辑

#### 段落模板

> **​[Topic sentence：核心发现（结论性陈述）]​**. We found that [具体数据/现象 + 统计比较] (Fig. X). [补充验证/量化/对照比较]. [弱归纳：correlates with / is consistent with 一句话关联]，[将解释和意义留给Discussion].

> **​⚠️ 关键修正**：原模板末句"These results indicate that [意义]"违反"只报发现不报解释"原则。`indicate`属于解释性动词，应删除或改为描述性关联表达；意义性归纳应移至Discussion部分。

#### 正确示范（√）

##### 案例一：器件效率比较

> **The 2D/3D interface significantly improves device efficiency.​** The 2D/3D device achieved a power conversion efficiency of 22.5% ± 0.3% (n = 10), significantly higher than the 2D-only (18.2% ± 0.4%, p < 0.001) and 3D-only (20.1% ± 0.3%) devices (Fig. 2a). The improvement is primarily due to an increase in fill factor, from 0.72 to 0.81 (Fig. 2b).

##### 案例二：稳定性提升

> **The 2D/3D interface significantly improves device stability.​** Devices with the 2D/3D interface retained 92% ± 2% of their initial efficiency after 1000 h of continuous illumination, whereas control devices retained only 45% ± 5% under identical conditions (Fig. 4a). This enhancement was reproducible across 20 independent devices (standard deviation < 5%, Fig. 4b).

##### 案例三：温度效应观察

> **Elevated temperature accelerates device degradation.​** At 60°C, the device efficiency dropped to 50% of its initial value within 100 h. In contrast, at 25°C, the device retained 90% efficiency after 1000 h (Fig. 5a). SEM imaging revealed pinhole formation at 60°C after 200 h, which was absent at 25°C (Fig. 5b). The decrease in efficiency correlates with the appearance of pinholes.

#### 错误示范（×）及修正对照

##### 问题一：混入解释（串台Discussion）

> **​× 错误：​** The higher efficiency of the 2D/3D device **suggests that** the 2D layer improves charge extraction.
>
> **​√ 修正：​** The 2D/3D device achieved 22.5% efficiency, higher than the control (18.2%).

##### 问题二：无主题句，直接扔图表

> **​× 错误：​** Figure 3 shows the J-V curves of devices with different annealing temperatures.
>
> **​√ 修正：​** **Higher annealing temperature improves device efficiency.​** The 150°C-annealed device achieved a PCE of 22%, compared to 18% for the 100°C-annealed device (Fig. 3).

##### 问题三：数据堆砌无统计无比较

> **​× 错误：​** The 2D/3D device had 22% efficiency, and the control had 18%. The 2D/3D device was more stable.
>
> **​√ 修正：​** The 2D/3D device achieved 22.5% ± 0.3% efficiency (n = 10), **significantly higher than** the control (18.2% ± 0.4%, p < 0.001, t-test). Moreover, the 2D/3D device retained 90% ± 2% after 500 h, **compared to** 45% ± 5% for controls.

##### 问题四：念数字代替结论

> **​× 错误：​** The 2D-only device has a Jsc of 22 mA/cm², Voc of 1.08 V, FF of 0.72, and PCE of 18.2%. The 3D-only device has Jsc of 23 mA/cm², Voc of 1.10 V, FF of 0.79, and PCE of 20.1%...
>
> **​√ 修正：​** The 2D/3D device achieved a PCE of 22.5%, significantly higher than the 2D-only (18.2%) and 3D-only (20.1%) devices (Fig. 2a). The improvement is primarily due to an increase in fill factor (Fig. 2b).

#### Results写作自检清单

| 检查项 | 要求 | 不通过时修改方式 |
|---|---|---|
| 主题句 | 段落第一句是否为明确结论 | 把结论提到第一句，不以"Fig X shows"开头 |
| 一段一结论 | 本段是否只讨论一个核心发现 | 拆分为两个段落 |
| 统计支撑 | 是否提供具体数据与统计（n, SD, p值） | 补充误差、p值、样本量 |
| 动词选择 | 是否避免了解释性动词（suggests, indicates） | 改为描述性动词（shows, reveals, is） |
| 意义陈述 | 是否避免了意义性判断（is important） | 删除或移至Discussion |
| 图表引用 | 引用是否清晰（图号 + 具体比较） | 明确"as shown in Fig. X"或括号引用 |
| 故事线 | 本段结论是否服务于全文逻辑 | 无关内容删除或移至Supplementary |
| 逻辑顺序 | 段落排列是否为逻辑顺序 | 改为宏观→微观/核心→辅助的逻辑 |

#### 检验标准

读完每个Results段落的第一句，就能知道整篇Results的所有核心结论。如果做不到，说明主题句缺失或结论不明确。



### 4.3 Discussion写作模板

Discussion的唯一任务：把Results从"事实"上升到"意义"，有分寸、有证据、有自知之明。

采用**倒漏斗结构**（从具体到宽泛，最后收束），理想长度3–5段，约为Results的1/2到2/3。

必须回答三个问题，对应三段：

---

**第1段：Why it matters**

> Our findings reveal that [核心发现], which [重要意义].

- 用1–2句话，从**更高的角度**重新表述核心发现
- **不要重复Results中的具体数字**，而是用概括性结论
- 回答"意味着什么"——你的发现如何填补了Introduction中提出的gap？它改变了什么理解？
- 如果需要提数据，用概括表达如"the observed 10-fold improvement"，而非"from 100 h to 1000 h"

**​❌ 错误（重复Results）：​**
> "Our results showed that the 2D/3D device achieved 22.5% efficiency and retained 90% after 1000 h."

**​✅ 正确（概括重申）：​**
> "Our results demonstrate that a 2D/3D perovskite interface simultaneously enhances device efficiency and stability, achieving a 10-fold improvement in operational lifetime."

---

**第2段：How it compares**

> Compared with previous studies showing [已有发现], our results [一致/不同/推进], suggesting that [新认知].

- 核心段落，必须与文献对话，至少引用3–5篇文献进行比较
- 先解释意义（1–2句），再与文献比较（2–3句）
- 明确写出具体作者或文献编号，不要用"some studies"这类模糊指代
- 比较时要具体说明：支持？反驳？还是补充？

**常用比较表达：​**

| 关系 | 表达 |
|------|------|
| 支持前人 | Consistent with… / In agreement with… / This corroborates… |
| 反驳前人 | In contrast to… / Contrary to… / This challenges the view that… |
| 补充前人 | Extending previous work… / We further show that… / In addition to… |

**​❌ 错误（空泛无比较）：​**
> "Our work is very important for the field. It shows that interfaces matter. This will help make better solar cells."

**​✅ 正确（有分寸、有比较）：​**
> "These findings identify interfacial degradation as a previously underestimated bottleneck in perovskite device stability. **Unlike** previous reports that focused on bulk composition engineering (ref 10–12), our work shows that the perovskite/HTL interface plays a dominant role in determining operational lifetime. **Consistent with** recent theoretical predictions (ref 13), we find that a 2D interlayer suppresses ion migration. **However,​** our observed 10-fold enhancement exceeds the 3–5 fold improvements reported in earlier experimental studies (ref 14–15), likely due to our optimized spacer cation."

**​⚠️ 避免空泛拔高：​** 不要用"revolutionize""breakthrough""enormous implications"等无证据支撑的夸张表达。改为有分寸的表达：
- "This identifies X as a previously unrecognized factor."
- "This finding suggests that…"
- "Our work provides a new strategy for…"

---

**第3段：Limitations**

> Several limitations should be noted. [局限1]. [局限2]. Future studies should [改进方向].

- 主动承认局限**不会**让论文被拒，反而显得成熟、严谨、可信
- 审稿人本来就会找茬，主动说出来反而堵住了他的嘴
- **不要写致命伤**（如"我们的数据不可重复"），写所有研究都会有的边界条件（测试条件、样本量、时间范围、材料纯度等）
- 每个局限后必须跟上具体的"未来可以怎么做"，不要写"More research is needed"这种废话
- 承认局限的同时，也要重申贡献，用"Nevertheless""Despite these limitations, our work shows…"来平衡

**​❌ 错误（回避局限/废话/过度谦虚）：​**
> "Our work has no limitations." — 不严谨
> "Further studies are needed to fully understand this phenomenon." — 等于没说
> "This is just a small step. We only tested one condition." — 过度谦虚

**​✅ 正确（具体局限+具体方向+重申贡献）：​**
> "This study has several limitations. First, our devices were tested only under continuous illumination, whereas real-world conditions involve diurnal cycles. Future work should test pulsed or solar-simulated profiles. Second, the 2D layer synthesis requires an extra processing step; developing a one-step deposition method would improve scalability. Finally, the long-term stability beyond 1000 h remains to be evaluated. **Nevertheless,​** our findings establish interfacial engineering as a promising route toward stable perovskite photovoltaics."

---

#### 完整案例：钙钛矿太阳能电池Discussion

> Our results demonstrate that a 2D/3D perovskite interface simultaneously enhances device efficiency and stability, achieving a 10-fold improvement in operational lifetime.
>
> These findings identify interfacial degradation as a critical bottleneck that has been overlooked in favor of bulk composition engineering (ref 10–12). **Consistent with** recent modeling studies (ref 13), our data show that the 2D layer suppresses ion migration. **However,​** the magnitude of stability enhancement we observe (10×) significantly exceeds the 3–5× improvements reported for additive-based strategies (ref 14–15). This discrepancy likely arises from the direct passivation of interfacial defects by our 2D layer, an effect not achieved by bulk additives. **Notably,​** our work also reveals a trade-off: thicker 2D layers (>10 nm) reduce efficiency despite further improving stability, suggesting an optimal thickness range.
>
> This study has limitations. Our stability tests were conducted under continuous illumination at fixed temperature, whereas real-world conditions involve diurnal cycles and temperature variations. Future work should employ ISOS protocols under more realistic conditions. Additionally, the long-term stability beyond 1000 h and the scalability to large-area modules remain to be demonstrated. **Nevertheless,​** our findings establish interfacial molecular engineering as a complementary strategy to compositional optimization for achieving stable, high-efficiency perovskite solar cells.

| 检查项 | 是否满足 | 未满足则修改 |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 第1段是否用1–2句概括了核心发现（不重复具体数字）？ | | 改写为概括性陈述 |
| 第2段是否解释了"意味着什么"（填补了什么gap）？ | | 加上"This identifies / reveals / establishes…" |
| 第2段是否与文献进行了比较（支持/反驳/补充）？ | | 至少引用3–5篇文献进行比较 |
| 第2段的比较是否有具体指向（不是"some studies"）？ | | 写出具体作者或文献编号 |
| 第3段是否明确写出了局限性（至少2点）？ | | 诚实列出测试条件、样本量、时间范围等边界 |
| 第3段的局限性后是否跟了"未来可以怎么做"？ | | 每个局限后加一句"Future work should…" |
| 是否避免了重复Results中的具体数字？ | | 删掉或改为概括表达 |
| 是否避免了空泛吹嘘（"revolutionary"）？ | | 改为有分寸的表达（"provides a new strategy"） |
| 是否避免了过度谦虚（"just a small step"）？ | | 用"Nevertheless / Despite these limitations"平衡 |


### 4.4 Abstract写作模板

**核心理念：​** Abstract不是论文的压缩版，而是**独立的说服工具**。写作前先问自己：**​"如果审稿人只读这200词，我希望他记住哪三件事？"​**

**检验标准：​** 一个外行读完你的Abstract，应能说出——你解决了什么问题、怎么解决的、发现了什么具体结果、为什么重要。

#### 五步法结构（总词数150–250，比例约1:1:1:3:1）

1. **Background**（1句）：[领域] is important because [原因].
   - 快速锚定子领域，说明为什么值得研究
   - **不要**写教科书式大背景（能源危机、气候变化等），1句直接切入
   - ✅ "Perovskite solar cells have achieved power conversion efficiencies exceeding 25%, positioning them as a leading next-generation photovoltaic technology."
   - ❌ "Energy is a big problem in the world. Solar energy is clean and abundant. Perovskite solar cells are a new type of solar cell."

2. **Gap**（1句）：However, [具体未解决的问题] remains unclear / remains a critical barrier.
   - 这是让Abstract产生"张力"的关键，必须指出**一个具体的**未解决问题
   - 用 **However / Yet** 引导，不要写"stability is still a problem"这类任何领域都能套用的空话
   - ✅ "However, their operational stability under real-world conditions remains a critical barrier to commercialization, and the degradation mechanisms at the perovskite/charge-transport-layer interface are poorly understood."
   - ❌ "However, stability is still a problem."

3. **Method**（1句）：Here, we [方法概述 + 独特策略] to investigate [问题].
   - 用1–2个关键词说明方法的**独特之处**，给读者一个"画面"
   - **不要**写"we fabricated and characterized"，要写具体的策略和手段
   - ✅ "Here, we design a 2D/3D perovskite interface to suppress interfacial degradation and systematically investigate its effect on device performance and stability using in situ photoluminescence mapping and device testing."
   - ❌ "In this work, we prepared perovskite solar cells and tested them."

4. **Result**（2–3句，信息最密集部分）：We show that [核心发现，必须有具体数字].
   - 这是Abstract中字数最多、信息最密集的部分
   - 每个关键结论**必须有数字**：绝对数值、变化百分比、或与对照组的比值
   - 用 **Notably / Furthermore** 连接多个发现
   - ✅ "We find that the 2D/3D interface improves efficiency from 18.2% (control) to 22.5% — a 4.3 percentage point increase. Notably, the 2D/3D devices retain 92% of their initial efficiency after 1000 hours of continuous illumination, whereas control devices retain only 45%. Furthermore, the optimal 2D layer thickness is 10 nm; thicker layers (20 nm) reduce efficiency despite further improving stability."
   - ❌ "The results show that the 2D/3D device has better efficiency and stability."

5. **Impact**（1句）：This finding identifies / establishes / provides [具体意义].
   - 说明发现**意味着什么**——填补了什么认知空白、提供了什么新策略
   - **不要**空泛吹嘘"very important / will revolutionize"
   - ✅ "These results identify interfacial degradation as a previously underestimated bottleneck and establish 2D/3D interfacial engineering as a complementary strategy to bulk optimization for achieving stable, high-efficiency perovskite solar cells."
   - ❌ "Our work is very important and will revolutionize the field."

#### 功能标识词速查

| 步骤 | 标识词 |
|------|--------|
| Gap | However, Yet |
| Method | Here, we |
| Result | We find that, We show that, Notably, Furthermore |
| Impact | These results identify / establish / provide / challenge |

#### 中国作者常见问题速查

| 问题 | 表现 | 修复方案 |
|------|------|----------|
| 背景太长 | 2–3句写能源危机等大背景 | 删到1句，直接切入子领域 |
| 结果无数字 | "better / higher / improved" | 加具体数值 + 对照组比较 |
| 贡献空泛 | "provides a new strategy" | 用"identifies X as Y"结构 |
| 方法模糊 | "fabricated and characterized" | 用1–2个关键词说明独特性 |

#### 完整案例：钙钛矿太阳能电池论文（约200词）

> Perovskite solar cells have achieved power conversion efficiencies exceeding 25%, positioning them as a leading next-generation photovoltaic technology. **However,​** their operational stability under real-world conditions remains a critical barrier to commercialization, and the degradation mechanisms at the perovskite/charge-transport-layer interface are poorly understood. **Here,​** we design a 2D/3D perovskite interface to suppress interfacial degradation and systematically investigate its effect on device performance and stability. We find that the 2D/3D interface improves efficiency from 18.2% (control) to 22.5% — a 4.3 percentage point increase. **Notably,​** the 2D/3D devices retain 92% of their initial efficiency after 1000 hours of continuous illumination, compared to only 45% for control devices. The optimal 2D layer thickness is 10 nm; thicker layers (20 nm) reduce efficiency despite further improving stability. **These results identify** interfacial degradation as a previously underestimated bottleneck and **establish** 2D/3D interfacial engineering as a complementary strategy for achieving stable, high-efficiency perovskite photovoltaics.

| 步骤 | 我的内容（1–2句话） |
|------|---------------------|
| 背景 | [领域重要性，1句] |
| 缺口 | [However, 具体未解决问题, 1句] |
| 方法 | [Here, 我们用什么独特方法, 1句] |
| 结果 | [核心发现1（数字）；核心发现2（数字）；核心发现3（可选）] |
| 影响 | [These results identify/establish/provide…] |


### 4.5 Title & Figure规则

**Title**：

- 简短（10–15词为佳，不超过20词）
- 信息密度高，包含核心发现或关键数字
- 格式：[核心发现] + [系统/材料]（结论型，推荐）
- 结论性：直接说出发现了什么，而非"我们研究了…"
- 可检索：包含领域关键词，让同行能搜到
- 禁止：空洞描述型标题（"A study on..."、"Effect of..."、"An investigation into..."）

#### 标题类型与案例

| 类型 | 结构 | 案例 |
|------|------|------|
| **结论型**（推荐） | [核心发现] + [系统/材料] | "A 2D/3D perovskite interface suppresses ion migration and extends device lifetime 10-fold" |
| **问题型**（较少） | [问题] + [解决方案暗示] | "What limits perovskite stability? The role of interfacial degradation" |
| **方法型**（一般） | [方法] + [研究对象] | "In situ photoluminescence mapping reveals interfacial degradation pathways in perovskite solar cells" |

## 模块五：高阶提升与修改系统

### 5.1 Nature级表达特征

- **核心认知转变：​** 不要问"这句话够不够正式？"，而要问"我能不能用更少的词、更简单的词，把同样的意思表达得更清楚？"
- **Nature级别表达 = 极简语言 + 极强逻辑 + 高信息密度**，不是词汇量大比拼，而是"删"的艺术
- **根本区别：​** 你认为"正式"= "复杂"，而Nature的标准是 **​"清晰"= "高级"​**
- 去除一切"废话句"（不能删除的句子才是必要的）
- 每句话要么推进论证，要么提供证据，否则删除

#### 四大支柱

**支柱1：极简语言 —— 删掉一切不必要的词**

能用1个词就不用2个词，能删就删。

| 废话表达（✗） | 简洁表达（✓） |
|---|---|
| It is worth noting that | Notably / 直接删 |
| It should be emphasized that | Importantly / 直接删 |
| It is well known that | 直接陈述（或加引用） |
| In the present study, we | Here, we / We |
| Due to the fact that | Because |
| In order to | To |
| There is a possibility that | May / Could |
| As a matter of fact | 直接删 |
| For the purpose of | For / To |
| At a temperature of 150°C | At 150°C |

#### 极简语言案例

> ✗ It is worth noting that, due to the fact that the temperature was increased, there was a significant increase in the reaction yield.
>
> ✓ Increasing the temperature increased the yield. / Higher temperature increased the yield.

**支柱2：结论前置 —— 开门见山，不绕弯子**

第一句话就给结论，不要把结论藏在段尾。

#### 结论前置案例

> ✗ We measured the efficiency of devices fabricated with different annealing temperatures. The device annealed at 150°C showed 22% efficiency, while the 100°C device showed 18%. Therefore, higher annealing temperature improves efficiency.
>
> ✓ Higher annealing temperature improves device efficiency. The 150°C-annealed device achieved 22% efficiency, compared to 18% for the 100°C device.

**应用到全文各部分：​**

- Abstract：第一句给出背景和缺口，第二句给出核心答案
- Introduction：第一段末尾或第二段开头给出"Here, we …"
- Results：每段第一句是主题句（结论）
- Discussion：第一句重申核心发现

**支柱3：高信息密度 —— 每个词都要传递信息**

没有"填充词"，每个词都承载具体信息。检验方法：逐词问自己"删掉这个词，意思会变弱吗？"如果不会，就删。

高频填充词（可删）：very, really, quite, rather, somewhat / in terms of, with respect to, regarding / it should be noted that, it is important that

#### 高信息密度案例

> ✗ The device that was fabricated with the 2D layer showed a very good performance in terms of efficiency.
>
> ✓ The 2D-layer device achieved 22.5% efficiency.

**支柱4：强动词驱动 —— 拒绝名词化和弱动词**

让动词承载主要动作，而不是把动作藏进名词。

| 弱表达 | 强表达 |
|---|---|
| had an effect on | affected / governed |
| showed an increase | increased |
| was in agreement with | agreed with |
| conducted an analysis | analyzed |
| gave a demonstration | demonstrated |
| resulted in an enhancement | enhanced |
| made an improvement | improved |

#### 强动词驱动案例

> ✗ The implementation of the 2D layer resulted in an enhancement of stability.
>
> ✓ The 2D layer enhanced stability.

#### 中国作者常见问题剖析

**问题1：过度使用"It is … that"结构**

直接说发现了什么，不用"It is … that"绕弯子。

> ✗ It was found that the 2D layer improves stability.
> ✓ The 2D layer improves stability.
>
> ✗ It can be seen from Figure 2 that efficiency increases with temperature.
> ✓ Efficiency increases with temperature (Fig. 2).

**问题2：冗余的"there be"结构**

> ✗ There is a significant difference between the two groups.
> ✓ The two groups differ significantly.
>
> ✗ There was a rapid increase in temperature after 10 minutes.
> ✓ Temperature increased rapidly after 10 minutes.

**问题3："which/that"从句过度使用**

一个句子最多一个which/that从句，超过就拆分。

> ✗ The device, which was fabricated using a 2D layer that was deposited by spin-coating, which is a standard technique in the field, showed high efficiency.
> ✓ The device showed high efficiency. It was fabricated using a 2D layer deposited by spin-coating, a standard technique.

**问题4：喜欢用"respectively"​**

能不用就不用，分别列出更清晰；列表很长时用表格或分句。

> ✗ Samples A, B, and C had efficiencies of 18%, 20%, and 22%, respectively.
> ✓ Sample A had 18% efficiency, sample B 20%, and sample C 22%.

#### Nature表达自检清单

写完一段文字后，逐句检查：

| 检查项 | 如果"否"，怎么改 |
|---|---|
| 能否删掉"It is worth noting that"等废话？ | 删掉或换成Notably |
| 能否删掉"there is/are"并改用主动动词？ | There is a difference → differ |
| 能否删掉"very/really/quite"等弱副词？ | 直接删掉 |
| 能否把名词化改成动词？ | make an improvement → improve |
| 是否每个句子都以主语开头？ | 重构句子 |
| 句长是否超过25词？ | 拆分成两个句子 |
| 每个词是否都在传递信息？ | 删掉填充词 |

**终极检验：​** 把你自己写的句子和Nature上同领域论文的句子并排对比，如果你的句子明显更长、更绕、更多废话，就继续删。


### 5.2 母语者写作训练法

不是模仿词汇，而是模仿：

- **句型**：收集Nature/Science中的高频句型并套用
- **信息顺序**：结论先行，再给证据和解释（英文习惯：结论 → 证据 → 解释；中文习惯：铺垫 → 转折 → 结论）
- **段落节奏**：长短句交替，短句（5-10词）制造力度和停顿，长句（15-25词）提供细节和流畅性
- **动词位置**：英文习惯主语短、动词靠前；避免中文式长主语拖延核心动词

#### 核心方法：Shadow Writing（精读 + 仿写）

1. **选文**：从Nature/Science找一篇你领域内的优秀论文
2. **精读**：选取2-3个段落，逐句拆解（句型、词数、连接方式、节奏）
3. **提取模板**：写下3-5个可复用的句型骨架
4. **仿写**：合上原文，用自己的研究内容套用同样句型结构写1-2段
5. **对比**：与原文对比，调整语序和用词

> 注意：仿写是模仿**结构**，不是抄袭**内容**。你是在学习"怎么写"，而不是"写什么"。

#### 精读拆解维度

| 维度 | 要问的问题 |
|------|-----------|
| 句型 | 主语是什么？动词是什么？信息顺序是怎样的？ |
| 句长 | 几个词？长短句如何交替？ |
| 连接 | 句子之间用了什么连接词？还是自然过渡？ |
| 信息密度 | 每个词是否都必要？有没有废话？ |
| 节奏 | 读起来是否有韵律感？停顿在哪里？ |

#### 典型中式问题与修正

| 问题 | 表现 | 修正方向 |
|------|------|---------|
| 只换词不改结构 | 用Thesaurus把"important"换成"crucial"，但句法仍中式 | 重点模仿主语选择和动词位置，让主语变短、动词提前 |
| 过度使用连接词 | "However… Moreover… Furthermore… Therefore…" 每句一个 | 模仿零连接词过渡，靠内容逻辑自然衔接 |
| 信息顺序中式 | 铺垫 → 转折 → 结论（结论在最后） | 结论先行，再给证据：结论 → 证据 → 解释 |
| 句子等长无节奏 | 连续10句都是15-20词，像机器输出 | 长短句交替，短句制造力度，长句承载细节 |

#### 案例1：精读拆解示例

Nature原文片段：

> "Perovskite solar cells have achieved power conversion efficiencies exceeding 25%. **However,​** their operational stability remains a critical barrier to commercialization. **Here,​** we show that a 2D/3D perovskite interface suppresses ion migration and extends device lifetime 10-fold."

拆解：

- 句1：短句（7词），陈述成就（背景）
- 句2：短句（9词），以"However"转折，指出缺口
- 句3：中等长度（15词），以"Here, we show that"引出核心发现，动词"suppresses"和"extends"并列
- 节奏：短-短-中，快-快-稍慢，形成张力

提取句型骨架：


#### 案例2：仿写对照（锂金属电池）

| 原文句子 | 句型骨架 | 仿写 |
|---------|---------|------|
| Perovskite solar cells have achieved efficiencies exceeding 25%. | [领域] have achieved [指标] exceeding [数值] | Lithium metal batteries have achieved energy densities exceeding 300 Wh/kg. |
| However, their stability remains a barrier. | However, [问题] remains a [障碍] | However, their cycle life remains a barrier. |
| Here, we show that a 2D interface suppresses ion migration. | Here, we show that [方法] [动词] [现象] | Here, we show that a fluorinated additive suppresses dendrite growth. |

#### 案例3：只换词 vs 模仿句法

**X 换了词但结构中式：​**

> "The fact that the 2D layer can suppress ion migration is very crucial for stability."

**​√ 模仿句法：​**

> "The 2D layer suppresses ion migration — a crucial function for stability."

区别：主语从冗长的"The fact that…"变为简洁的"The 2D layer"，核心动词suppresses提前，信息密度提高。

#### 案例4：信息顺序修正

**X 中式顺序（铺垫在前，结论在后）：​**

> "When we increased the annealing temperature to 150°C, the grain size became larger, and as a result, we observed a higher efficiency."

**​√ 英文顺序（结论先行）：​**

> "Higher annealing temperature (150°C) increased grain size and efficiency."

#### 案例5：段落节奏修正

**X 等长句子无节奏：​**

> "The 2D layer suppresses ion migration. Stability improves as a result. Efficiency also increases significantly. Thicker layers reduce device performance."

**​√ 长短交替有节奏：​**

> "The 2D layer suppresses ion migration. (短，力度) This is a key finding. (短) Under continuous illumination, devices with the 2D/3D interface retained 92% of their initial efficiency after 1000 hours, compared to only 45% for controls. (长，信息密集) Thus, interfacial engineering is critical. (短，收束)"

#### 高频句型库（从Nature论文提取）

**引出本文工作：​**

- "Here, we show that …"
- "We demonstrate that …"
- "To address this, we developed …"
- "Here, we report …"

**报告结果：​**

- "We find that …"
- "Notably, …"
- "This leads to …"
- "Consistent with this, …"

**与文献比较：​**

- "In contrast to previous reports, …"
- "Consistent with X et al., we observe …"
- "This finding challenges the view that …"

**指出意义：​**

- "These results identify … as …"
- "This work establishes …"
- "Our findings suggest that …"

> 注意：不要只记单词替换，要记**整个短语的骨架**，包括标点和位置。



### 5.3 三轮修改法（必须按顺序执行）

> **核心原则：​** 结构决定逻辑，逻辑决定语言。从大到小，从粗到细，才是高效修改。不要问"这个句子语法对吗？"，而要问**​"如果我只改这一处，全文的整体逻辑会变好吗？"​**

> **时间分配：​** 第一轮结构40% → 第二轮逻辑30% → 第三轮语言30%

> **警告：​** 千万不要跳过第一轮、第二轮直接进入第三轮。结构没调好，语言改得再漂亮也是白费。

---

**第一轮：改结构（从宏观入手，打印出来用笔划）​**

- 主线是否清晰？是否能用一句话概括全文核心发现？
  - 删掉与主线无关的段落，移到Supplementary Information
  - 只读每段第一句，如果这些首句无法构成完整故事，说明段落主题句缺失或混乱
- 每个段落是否有明确功能？段落间逻辑是否递进？
  - 用不同颜色的笔标注各部分功能（背景/缺口/方法/结果/解释/意义），无明确功能的段落删或改
- Introduction的gap是否具体？是否在2-3段内建立了尖锐的gap？是否以"Here, we…"明确写出本文工作？
  - 把背景压缩到5句以内，明确写出gap和本文工作
- Results是否按逻辑顺序（而非时间顺序）排列？每段是否有主题句？
  - 重新排序图表，给每段加上主题句
- Discussion是否回答了三问（文献比较、机制解释、局限性）？是否避免了重复Results？
  - 删掉重复内容，加上比较和局限
- Abstract是否包含五步（背景-缺口-方法-结果-影响）？
  - 按五步法重写

#### ❌ 舍不得删的案例

Results里有5个图，图3和图4与主线关系不大，但作者觉得"数据挺好看"，硬塞在正文。

#### ✅ 果断删或移的案例

把图3和图4移到Supplementary，正文只保留图1、2、5。正文更聚焦，审稿人更容易理解主线。

---

**第二轮：改逻辑（从中观入手，逐段检查）​**

- 每个论断是否有证据支撑？
  - 每段是否有"主题句→证据→解释"结构？主题句放段首，证据紧随其后，最后加解释或过渡
  - 加上数字、n数、p值、图表引用
- 逻辑链条是否完整（A→B→C，不能跳步）？
  - 补上中间步骤或论证
- 连接词是否准确反映了段落/句子间的真实逻辑关系？
  - 段与段之间是自然过渡还是机械地用"However, …"？用逻辑连接词（因果、递进、转折）或靠内容自然过渡
- 同一个观点是否在多处重复？
  - 删掉重复处，只保留最合适的位置

#### 反向大纲法案例

为每段写一个一句话总结（大纲），检查这些大纲之间是否有逻辑顺序。如果某段的大纲与前后无关，就移动或删除。

#### 出声读案例

把论文读出来（或让别人读给你听）。哪里卡住了、哪里喘不过气，哪里就是逻辑或节奏的问题。

---

**第三轮：改语言（从微观入手，逐句检查）​**

- 句子是否≤20词？超过的必须拆解
  - 超过25词的句子拆分成2-3个短句
- 是否存在Chinese English问题？逐一排查：
  - "Although… but" → 删掉"but"
  - "Because… so" → 删掉"so"
- 是否名词化？逐一改为动词驱动
  - "make an improvement" → "improve"
- 是否有废话句？逐一删除
  - "It is worth noting that" → 直接删除
  - "due to the fact that" → 替换为"because"
- 是否过度使用被动语态？
  - 改为主动，除非Methods部分
- 是否过度使用"However, Moreover, Therefore"？
  - 替换或删除
- 语法/拼写是否正确？
  - 用Grammarly或请人校对
- 格式是否统一（参考文献、缩写、数字格式）？
  - 按期刊要求统一

#### 从后往前读案例

从最后一个句子往前读，跳出逻辑流，专注于语言本身的问题。写完初稿后放1-2天再以新鲜视角读一遍，很多问题会自动浮现。

---

**常见错误与解决方案**

| 常见错误 | 表现 | 解决方案 |
|---|---|---|
| 一上来就改语法 | 初稿写完立刻逐句润色，后来发现要删段落，所有润色白费 | 严格遵守三轮顺序：结构→逻辑→语言，结构和逻辑未定稿前不动语言 |
| 舍不得删 | "这数据我花了三个月，怎么能删？" | 问自己："删掉这个，核心故事会变弱吗？"不会就删，次要内容移到Supplementary |
| 自己看不出问题 | 自己读了10遍觉得完美，审稿人指出一堆逻辑问题 | 找"新鲜眼睛"——导师、同事、非本领域朋友，让他们指出哪里读不懂 |
| 修改没有记录 | Word里直接改，分不清最终版，出现"v5_final真的最终版v3.docx" | 使用版本控制，命名规则：`论文题目_v1_20260113`、`论文题目_v2_20260115` |

**终极检验：​** 修改完三轮后，找一位非本领域的朋友读Abstract和标题。如果他能说出"你解决了什么问题、发现了什么具体结果、为什么重要"，你就成功了。


### 5.4 完稿Checklist
完成三轮修改后，逐项检查：

- [ ] 能否用一句话说出论文的核心发现？
- [ ] Introduction的gap是否具体且有说服力？
- [ ] 每个Results段落是否以主题句开头？
- [ ] Discussion是否覆盖了意义、比较、局限？
- [ ] Abstract是否包含Background→Gap→Method→Result→Impact？
- [ ] Title是否≤15词且信息密度高？
- [ ] 平均句长是否在15-20词？
- [ ] 是否已清除所有Chinese English问题？
- [ ] 是否已清除所有名词化表达？
- [ ] 是否每句话都推进论证或提供证据？

## 交互模式

当科研人员寻求帮助时，按以下流程引导：

### 流程A：从零开始写论文
1. 先询问：你的研究核心发现是什么？（用一句话描述）
2. 基于核心发现，帮助设计故事线（Question→Gap→Method→Finding→Impact）
3. 按模块四的模板，逐部分引导撰写
4. 每写完一部分，立即用对应规则检查并修改
5. 全部完成后，执行三轮修改法和Checklist

### 流程B：修改已有稿件
1. 先执行第一轮（结构）诊断，指出结构性问题
2. 再执行第二轮（逻辑）诊断，标注逻辑断裂处
3. 最后执行第三轮（语言）诊断，逐句优化
4. 每处修改必须说明：改了什么、为什么改、依据本Skill哪条规则

### 流程C：单点咨询
- 如果科研人员只问某个具体问题（如"帮我改这段Results"），直接应用对应模块的规则进行修改，并引用规则编号
- 修改时必须给出：❌原文 → ✅改写 → 📝修改理由

---

## 输出规范

所有修改和建议必须遵循以下格式：

1. **诊断**：指出问题所属模块和规则编号
2. **改写**：❌原文 → ✅改写
3. **理由**：依据本Skill的哪条规则，为什么这样改更好
4. **进阶提示**：如果适用，给出进一步优化的方向

禁止：
- 只给出修改结果而不解释原因
- 使用模糊评价（如"这里写得不太好"）而不给出具体规则依据
- 跳过结构/逻辑问题直接修改语言
